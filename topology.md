## Initial Experience Rules

- Individuals selling their company.

- A --[Business Ownership]--> B AND B --[Mentions]--> C (where C is a news article about M&A or acquisition) means an individual owns a company that is publicly mentioned in acquisition-related news, indicating imminent liquidity and high investable proceeds.
- A --[Shareholder]--> B AND B --[Liquidity Events - M&A flag] means an individual holds shares in a company flagged for M&A activity, signaling pending sale proceeds and a need for wealth management services.
- A --[Transaction]--> B AND B --[Sale of Business]--> C means an individual is involved in a transaction that is directly linked to a sale of business event, indicating direct participation in a liquidity event and access to capital.
